About
DJ Natasha’s deep emotional and spiritual connection to music fuels her approach to DJing—sharing immersive, story-driven sets with anyone willing to listen. A natural leftfield selector, she uncovers pop sensibility within underground music, gravitating toward unexpected tracks and emotional through-lines rather than predictable moments.


In 2006, a chance opportunity led her to the decks. With an intuitive ear and deep musical knowledge, she taught herself to mix vinyl and began spinning at a beloved St. Louis hangout.


By 2009, DJ Natasha was thriving in Los Angeles—balancing a music supervision internship while performing at magazine launches and fashion shows alongside her female DJ collective. She curated themed nights and played across posh hotel lounges, nightclubs, and dive bars from Hollywood to Venice Beach.


After an over-a-decade hiatus, she’s back on deck, wax in hand, with creative freedom shaped by years of digital experimentation. Today, her sound blends long, hypnotic house transitions with indie dance grooves rooted in her original vinyl catalogue.


Drawn to contrast and texture, Natasha describes her sound as Disco Kitchen: a genre-blurring blend of underground disco, house, and indie dance—where pop sensibility meets deep, leftfield grooves. Her sets feature tropical percussion, piano-driven moments, acid-tinged synths, and moody post-punk influences woven into emotionally driven, story-forward dance floors.


Since arriving in Boston in late 2019, DJ Natasha has performed at local events including Dancing on the Charles, LoveStrukk, Groove, Dig Deep, and other East Coast Underground gatherings. She also occasionally streams live via Boston Upstream and her own Twitch channel.
