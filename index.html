// The Artist

Unexpected tracks.
Emotional through-lines.

DJ Natasha's deep emotional and spiritual connection to music fuels her approach to DJing—sharing immersive, story-driven sets with anyone willing to listen.

A natural leftfield selector, she uncovers pop sensibility within underground music, gravitating toward unexpected tracks and emotional through-lines rather than predictable moments.
